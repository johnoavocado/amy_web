Flying Cheese Brigade

Packaging, Illustration

  1. The Flying Cheese Brigade sell cheese at a market stall in the Limerick milk market
  2. The Flying Cheese Brigade, whose name was inspired by the wacky humour of Monty Python, believe that one of the most important things to them is “having fun”. Because of this I created a variety of funny, colourful illustrations which relate back to things to do with the company.
