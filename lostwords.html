Lost Words

Photography, Poster

  1. The result of a two hour workshop. Undertaken in groups of two.
  2. Each pair was given a word which is no longer comonly used in modern day language, and create a poster based on it. The meaning of 'nubivagant' is moving through clouds. The concept was based around a persons headspace when their head is 'in the clouds'. The poster was printed on a tissue-like light stock which would flutter when passed with movement. The words of William Wordsworth's 'Daffodils' were also added as the words 'I wandered lonely as a cloud' encaptured our idea perfectly.
